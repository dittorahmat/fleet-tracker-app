# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Fleet managers, dispatchers, and control room operators monitoring cargo trucks and delivery vehicles in real-time.

## Product Purpose
To provide instant, high-fidelity tracking of vehicle coordinates, speeds, and heading directions. The system facilitates immediate operational decisions and triggers real-time alerts for speeding or geofence breaches to maintain safety and efficiency.

## Brand Personality
Utility-focused, high-contrast, clean. A premium dark control room aesthetic designed for low-light environments.

## Anti-references
- Soft, low-contrast pastel dashboards.
- Overly decorated SaaS templates with excessive gradients and nested card shadows.
- High-latency polling UIs.

## Design Principles
1. **Real-time First**: Telemetry updates must be pushed instantly and rendered without blocking interactions.
2. **Alert Dominance**: Crucial violations (overspeeding, geofence breaches) must command visual attention with high-contrast, clear indicators.
3. **Data Density without Clutter**: Maximize readable screen real-estate with tight margins and clean lines, perfect for large control-room monitors.
4. **Contrast Integrity**: High contrast elements against a deep slate background for legibility under dim ambient lighting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios exceeding WCAG AA requirements (4.5:1 minimum for body text, 3:1 for large display metrics).
- Color-blind friendly warning badges (pairing colors with icons and explicit text labels, not just color coding).
- Option to toggle or pause animated routes for users sensitive to motion.
